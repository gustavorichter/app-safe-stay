Features

Everything you need to travel safely

Multiple detection layers to identify hidden surveillance devices.

Network Scanner

Scans all devices on the Wi-Fi network and flags IP addresses with ports typical of IP cameras, DVRs, and NVRs.

Magnetic Detector

Uses the phone's magnetometer to detect anomalous magnetic fields caused by hidden camera lenses or electronics.

Bluetooth Scanner

Lists nearby Bluetooth devices and highlights suspicious names associated with known spy cameras.

Camera Analysis

Helps identify suspicious lenses using the device camera with infrared reflection detection.
